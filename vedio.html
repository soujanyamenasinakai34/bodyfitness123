Coaching Tips

  • Start with a warm-up to prepare your body.
  • Focus on your form to avoid injuries.
  • Set realistic goals and track your progress.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a balanced diet.
  • Rest and recover adequately between workouts.
  • Avoid adding sugar to your food or drink
  • Stick to 3 square meals a day (breakfast, lunch, dinner) Reduce your portion size, especially at dinner. Use a standard 9 inch plate and aim to increase your portion of vegetables to at least half the dinner plate.
