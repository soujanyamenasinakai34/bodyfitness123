Cardio Exercises

Cardio exercises are great for burning calories and improving heart health. Here are some examples:

  • Running
  • Cycling
  • Jumping Rope
  • Swimming

Strength Training

Strength training helps build muscle and improve overall strength. Consider these exercises:

  • Weight Lifting
  • Push-Ups
  • Squats
  • Deadlifts

Flexibility Exercises

Flexibility exercises help improve the range of motion in your joints. Try these:

  • Yoga
  • Stretching
  • Pilates
  • Tai Chi
